Infra 12%, Ops 10%, Dev 10% are estimates. We agree on actuals before signing and review quarterly. Zero fixed cost — we don't eat. We work for the goal.

Starting price points and pack sizes. ×8 coefficient is the opening anchor — adjustable based on market testing, and could be way more: right now 1 hour of active tutor usage (photos + audio) costs the client ~$2–3.
Reporting & source of truth
Monthly reports by the 10th. App Store Connect is the canonical revenue source. Andrii gets read access.

Governing law and how the commission is paid (bank transfer, crypto, etc.). To be agreed.
