¿Cómo funciona?

Learn Spanish, doggo style.

Professor Madrid blends AI tutoring, verb conjugation drills, and audio loops into one surprisingly fun app. Your professor is a dog — he means well, mostly.

Daily Motivation
A new dark-humour quote to kick off every session
Two Learning Modes
Chat Tutor or Verb Game — you pick the battlefield
AI Chat Tutor
Conversational AI that teaches in Spanish from the first word
Seagull Steven 🦜
Audio loops of your chat — listen, repeat, get fluent
Spin the Wheel
Three verbs picked by a slot machine — fate decides your lesson
Conjugation Game
Speak the forms aloud. Beat the clock. Don't embarrass the professor.
