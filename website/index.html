How it works

Learn visually — like children do,
repeat and memorise like a dog,
play like a Spanish guitar

01

Street view — learn anywhere!

  1. Take a picture of what you see around
  2. Professor will describe every object in it
  3. Learn by talking with the app about what you see around

This is how children do — they remember what they see! And they always have someone to talk about it.

02

Consistency is the trick

  1. You get a personal schedule for every word and tons of smart notifications

This is how dogs learn to ride a bicycle.. Your Spanish is way easier, you can do it!

03

Verbs and times is 80% of Spanish

  1. Practice verbally — this is NLP
  2. Yo como, tu comes, el/ella come.. — remember this, it's 80% of the Spanish language
  3. Train reaction, not the knowledge, like dogs do

This is how sportsmen do — maximise number of verbal repetitions, because your goal is to SPEAK SPANISH (not just to know it)
