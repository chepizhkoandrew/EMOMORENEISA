Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data Professor Madrid ("we", "us") collects, why, and what choices you have. It applies to the Professor Madrid mobile app and this website.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data — when you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a unique identifier and, where you allow it, your name and email address.
  • Learning content — your chats with the tutor, words and topics you study, progress, and a learning profile we build to personalise lessons.
  • Usage & billing data — your treat balance, purchases, and a record of which features you used and when, so the app works and we can monitor costs.
  • Technical data — basic device and app information and logs needed for security, debugging, and reliability.

We do not sell your personal data.

2. How we use your data

  • To provide and personalise the Service (generate lessons, voice, drills).
  • To operate the treats and billing system and prevent abuse.
  • To maintain security, fix problems, and improve the Service.
  • To communicate with you about support or important changes.

3. Service providers we share data with

To run the Service we use trusted processors who handle data on our behalf. We share only what is needed for each to do its job:

ProviderPurpose
SupabaseAccount, learning data, treat balances and transactions

Content sent to AI providers is used to produce your results. We do not authorise these providers to use your content to train their models where an opt-out is available to us.

4. Cookies and the website

This website uses only essential cookies/local storage needed to remember that you have seen our cookie notice and to load fonts. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies on this site. The mobile app does not use advertising trackers.

5. Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active and as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You can ask us to delete your account and associated data (see Section 7).

6. Security

API keys and AI requests are handled by our server, not stored in the app, and traffic is encrypted in transit. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, email privacy@professormadrid.com. You also have the right to complain to your local data-protection authority.

8. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent where you live). We do not knowingly collect data from children below that age. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International transfers

Our providers may process data in countries other than yours, including the United States. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date and, for material changes, notify you in the app where appropriate.

11. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email privacy@professormadrid.com.
