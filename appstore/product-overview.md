# Professor Madrid — Product Overview

## The One-Line Pitch

**Professor Madrid is the first Spanish learning app that replaces a real private tutor — not by simulating one, but by being one.**

---

## What Makes This Different

Every Spanish app on the market teaches you to recognize words. Professor Madrid teaches you to *use* them — the same way a human tutor would: through real conversation, instant voice feedback, contextual pattern drilling, and an AI that adapts to exactly what you need in real time.

This is not a GPT wrapper with a language learning skin. The app uses:
- **State-of-the-art voice recognition** tuned for Spanish learner pronunciation — including errors, hesitations, and mixed-language input
- **Advanced vision models** that replicate the exact way children acquire language — through real visual context, not abstract flashcards
- **Frontier conversation models** that maintain full context across sessions, remembering what you've learned, where you struggled, and how you like to be taught
- **"Seagull Steven" audio engine** — a neuro-repetition system that uses involuntary auditory memory formation to make phrases stick whether you're consciously trying or not

The benchmark isn't Duolingo. It's a €50/hour native Spanish tutor. And Professor Madrid wins.

## Full User Flow

### 1. The Home Screen

The app opens on Professor Madrid himself — a real brown-and-white dog who is your professor, mascot, and motivational spirit animal. He appears with a speech bubble in Spanish: *"Vamos a enseñar me"* — and alongside him, the two paths into learning:

- **CHAT TUTOR** — blue, conversational, intelligent
- **VERB GAME** — golden, fast, mechanical

The home screen also shows floating ambient objects (food, money, props from Spanish life) and witty professor-style quotes that appear between sessions, like: *"They say fluent speakers earn more, travel further, and are eventually able to afford one extra coffee per week."*

---

### 2. Starting a Session — The Chat Tutor

**Step 1: Choose your learning style for this session**

The professor asks: *"How do you want to learn?"* and presents two options:

- **Choose a topic** — you name any area of Spanish grammar or vocabulary; the AI builds a full tutor session around it
- **Street View** — you photograph what's around you and the AI builds the lesson from your real environment

This choice is made fresh each session. The sessions are saved and resumable, shown in a history list with the first line of each lesson as the title.

---

**Step 2a: Topic Mode — Text & Voice Conversations**

You type or *speak* your learning goal. The input screen shows example prompts and quick-pick topic chips:
- "Past-tense verbs"
- "Spanish song"
- "I like, I don't like…"
- "Spatial adverbs (of place)"
- "Palabrotas…" *(yes, swear words are a topic)*

You can speak your request directly using the microphone — the speech recognition picks up conversational input, not just clean dictation.

The AI opens a lesson in the chat interface. It teaches like a real tutor: it introduces new vocabulary in context, shows example sentences, and immediately challenges you to use the words yourself. An example response from the tutor:

> *"Vamos con tres verbos nuevos: vivir (to live), escribir (to write) y leer (to read). Fíjate en cómo los usamos: Yo vivo en Madrid. Tú escribes una carta. Ella lee un libro. Ahora, dime en español: I live in London, you write a message, he reads the newspaper."*

This is not a vocabulary list. This is a tutor session.

In the chat you can:
- Reply by **typing** your answer in Spanish
- Reply by **speaking** — the microphone button activates voice recognition
- Send **camera photos** or pick from your **photo library** mid-conversation
- **Listen to every message read aloud** — the tutor speaks, not just types. Each message has a playback button and an inline audio scrubber so you can rewind to exactly the word you want
- **Access Seagull Steven** on any message — tap the parrot to enter neuro-repetition mode and loop the phrase until it's in your bones

Every session is fully recorded. Every message is replayable. You can return to any past lesson and listen back to the tutor's voice, replay your own answers, or send any phrase to Seagull Steven for another round of drilling. Your learning is never disposable.

---

**Step 2b: Street View Mode — Visual Learning**

You can add up to 4 photos of your real surroundings — your desk, your kitchen, a café, the street outside — and the AI analyzes them using a vision model to identify objects, build vocabulary around what it sees, and open a contextual conversation lesson.

The tagline on this screen: *"Visual learning: remember words 5× faster from photos of your real life."*

**This is not a feature. It's a methodology.**

There is decades of research into how children acquire their first language. They don't sit with flashcards. They point at things, hear the word, and the word fuses to the memory of the object — the color of it, the texture, where it was in the room. That sensory-contextual binding is why native speakers don't have to *think* about words. The words just appear, because they're attached to lived experience.

Every other language app ignores this completely. They give you a drawing of a cup and the word "taza." Professor Madrid gives you a photo of *your* cup, on *your* table, in the place where you drink coffee every morning — and teaches you *that* word. The AI sees what you see. The lesson is built from your world. And your brain stores it the same way it stored your first language: not as a fact to recall, but as a thing you know.

---

### 3. Seagull Steven — The Neuro-Repetition Engine

**Seagull Steven** (The Spanish Parrot) is one of the most distinctive features of Professor Madrid. It surfaces from inside the chat session whenever you want to drill a specific phrase at the neurological level.

**How it works:**

Every message in the chat tutor is a real voice recording — the AI speaks its lessons out loud. Every one of those recordings is saved and replayable directly from the conversation, at any time. You can go back to a lesson from three days ago and hear it exactly as the tutor delivered it.

But Seagull Steven goes further. When you tap the parrot icon on any message, Seagull Steven takes that phrase and enters **neuro-repetition mode**.

A warming-up screen shows: *"Seagull Steven is warming up… Recording segment 1 of 7…"*

Once ready, you get a full immersion interface:
- The Spanish phrase is displayed word by word — each word highlighted in sequence as the audio plays, like a karaoke track in your second language
- A repeat loop selector: **1× 2× 4× 6× 8× 10× 20×** — you choose how deep to drill
- Full playback controls: skip, rewind, pause, restart
- A **"Talk to Parrot"** button — you speak the phrase back, and the AI evaluates your pronunciation against the original recording

**Why repetition at this scale works:**

The human brain does not decide what to keep. It keeps what it hears repeatedly. This is not a motivational principle — it is how auditory memory is physically formed. Each time a phrase loops, the neural pathway that stores it gets slightly stronger. At 20 repetitions of a 7-second clip, you've had 140 seconds of exposure to one phrase spoken in natural Spanish. You will remember it. Not because you tried. Because you're a human with ears and a brain, and that's what brains do.

Seagull Steven doesn't ask you to memorize. It just keeps playing. Like a parrot.

### 4. The Verb Game — Slot Machine + Conjugation Matrix

**The Verb Game** is where grammar gets drilled mechanically. It runs as a separate mode from the home screen.

**Step 1: The Slot Machine Verb Draw**

A golden casino-style slot machine with three spinning reels selects 3 random Spanish verbs from the database. The reels are animated with colorful fruit symbols and stop one by one, landing on the 3 verbs that define your round. Example: **vivir** (-ir), **responder** (-er), **preparar** (-ar).

This gamification of verb selection serves a real pedagogical purpose: it forces the learner to work with all three conjugation families (-ar, -er, -ir) simultaneously rather than drilling one pattern in isolation.

**Step 2: The Conjugation Grid**

A 3×6 matrix appears:
- **Columns**: the 3 selected verbs
- **Rows**: the 6 Spanish pronouns — yo, tú, él/ella, nosotros, vosotros, ellos/ellas

Each cell is initially hidden (shown as **?**). The game calls out a cell by displaying the prompt at the top — e.g., *"NOSOTROS RESPOND…"* — and you speak or type the correct conjugated form.

Visual feedback is instant and unambiguous:
- **Green checkmark** = correct
- **Red X + the correct answer** = wrong — you see immediately what it should have been

The active cell pulses with an animated microphone icon when voice input is expected. The timer arc around the top prompt bar shows how long you've been on the current challenge.

This single game mechanic — randomized verbs, full pronoun matrix, voice-first input, instant correction — is more effective for conjugation mastery than hours of traditional drilling.

## Session History & Continuity

All conversations are saved automatically and shown in a history list. Each entry shows:
- A mode icon (chat vs. visual/camera)
- The opening line of the session as its title (e.g., "Let's learn some greetings")
- A timestamp (26 minutes ago, 3 hours ago, 1 day ago)

Sessions are fully resumable. You can pick up any conversation exactly where you left off, and the AI remembers the context — what was taught, what you struggled with, what you got right.

## Target User

**Primary**: Adults (25–45) who want real Spanish skills for travel, work, or personal goals — and have already been disappointed by apps that feel like children's games.

**Secondary**: Students using it to supplement classroom learning. The app has no shame about covering "Palabrotas" (colorful vocabulary) — which means it's designed for real adults, not a sanitized educational environment.

**The core insight**: The people who most need to learn a language are the ones who least have time for gamified nonsense. Professor Madrid respects your time and your intelligence.
