Meet Professor Madrid — the first Spanish learning app that doesn't just simulate a tutor. It is one.

Forget flashcards and streak gamification. Professor Madrid uses the same frontier AI models that power the world's most advanced language research — packed into a personal Spanish tutor that's available 24/7, adapts to you in real time, and actually teaches you to speak.

*** CHAT TUTOR ***
Have a real conversation in Spanish. Text, voice, or both. Ask anything — grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, culture. The Professor remembers what you've learned, how you learn, and what you're still getting wrong.

Every message is a real voice recording. Tap any bubble to replay it. The Professor's voice is your model — listen, repeat, and nail the pronunciation.

*** VISUAL MODE (STREET VIEW) ***
This isn't a gimmick. It's the exact methodology proven to work in child language acquisition: learn words by seeing the real thing. Photograph up to 4 objects around you — your kitchen, your office, your street — and Professor Madrid builds a full vocabulary lesson from your actual environment.

The same contextual binding that helps children absorb thousands of words before age five, now available in your pocket.

*** SEAGULL STEVEN ***
Language stays in your brain through repetition. Seagull Steven, the Professor's loyal companion, loops any phrase up to 20 times with karaoke-style word highlighting — creating involuntary auditory memory whether you're actively studying or not.

It's not just listening. It's neuroscience-backed repetition that makes phrases stick even when you're not trying.

*** VERB GAME ***
Spanish conjugation is the wall every learner hits. The Verb Game spins three random verbs from the full -ar/-er/-ir spectrum, presents a 3x6 conjugation matrix, and takes your answers by voice. Green. Red. Try again. No mercy, no hints — just the mechanical drilling that builds true grammar fluency.
