*** STREET VIEW — LEARN ANYWHERE ***
This isn't a gimmick. It's the exact methodology proven to work in child language acquisition: learn words by seeing the real thing. Photograph up to 4 objects around you — your kitchen, your office, your street — and Professor Madrid builds a full vocabulary lesson from your actual environment.

This is how children do — they remember what they see. The same contextual binding that helps children absorb thousands of words before age five, now available in your pocket.

*** CHAT TUTOR ***
Have a real conversation in Spanish. Text, voice, or both. Ask anything — grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, culture. The Professor remembers what you've learned, how you learn, and what you're still getting wrong.

Every message is a real voice recording. Tap any bubble to replay it. The Professor's voice is your model — listen, repeat, and nail the pronunciation.

*** EL LORO ***
Language stays in your brain through repetition. El Loro, the Professor's loyal companion, loops any phrase up to 20 times with karaoke-style word highlighting — creating involuntary auditory memory whether you're actively studying or not.

This is how dogs learn. Your Spanish is way easier, you can do it.

*** VERB GAME ***
Verbs and times is 80% of Spanish. The Verb Game spins three random verbs from the full -ar/-er/-ir spectrum, presents a 3x6 conjugation matrix, and takes your answers by voice. Green. Red. Try again. No mercy, no hints — train reaction, not the knowledge.
