New: Roleplay — a live podcast with Professor Madrid

Pick a guest (a historical figure, a mythical character, anyone with personality) and a setting, and step into a three-way Spanish conversation. Madrid hosts, your guest plays their part, and you join right in — the conversation flows naturally, so who speaks next isn't always the same. Each episode gets its own illustrated scene and a distinct voice for your guest.

New: Friends & song sharing

Invite a friend by email, and once you're connected, share any song you've created together.

Also new: Remember with Music — turn your vocabulary into a song. Pick a genre and some words from your memory queue, and Professor Madrid writes and sings a short original Spanish song built around them, played back karaoke-style with synced lyrics and illustrated pictures.

We also reorganized the home menu for clearer, more intuitive navigation between Speaking, Memory, and Grammar.
